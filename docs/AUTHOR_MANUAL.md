# Author Manual

This guide covers the article submission and revision process for authors.

## Overview

Authors can:
- Submit manuscripts for peer review
- Track submission status
- Respond to reviewer feedback
- Submit revisions
- View published articles

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Go to the registration page (`/auth/register`)
2. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email address
   - Password
   - Affiliation (institution/organization)
3. Click "Register"
4. You can now log in and submit manuscripts

### Logging In

1. Go to `/auth/login`
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"

## Submitting a Manuscript

### Preparing Your Manuscript

Before submitting, ensure your manuscript:
- Is in **PDF format**
- Follows the [Author Guidelines](/submit)
- Includes all figures and tables
- Is anonymized for blind review (remove author names from PDF)

### Submission Process

1. Log in to your account
2. Go to **Submit Manuscript** (`/dashboard/submit`)
3. Fill in the submission form:

   **Title** (required)
   - Clear, descriptive title
   - Max 200 characters

   **Abstract** (required)
   - Structured abstract (Background, Methods, Results, Conclusions)
   - Up to 300 words

   **Keywords** (required)
   - 3-6 keywords, comma-separated
   - Help readers find your article

   **Manuscript PDF** (required)
   - Upload your anonymized manuscript
   - Max file size: 10MB

   **Co-Authors** (optional)
   - Add names and affiliations of co-authors

4. Click "Submit Manuscript"
5. You'll receive a confirmation notification

## Tracking Your Submissions

### Viewing Submissions

Go to **My Submissions** (`/dashboard/my-submissions`)

You'll see all your submissions with:
- Title
- Current status
- Submission date
- Available actions

### Understanding Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| SUBMITTED | Under editorial review |
| UNDER_REVIEW | Sent to peer reviewers |
| REVISION_REQUESTED | Needs revision (see feedback) |
| RESUBMITTED | Revision received, under review |
| ACCEPTED | Accepted for publication |
| REJECTED | Not accepted (see feedback) |
| PUBLISHED | Available on the public site |

## Responding to Decisions

### If Revision is Requested

1. Go to My Submissions
2. Find the article with "REVISION_REQUESTED" status
3. Click "View" to see reviewer feedback
4. Click "Submit Revision"
5. On the revision page, you'll see:
   - Current revision number
   - Previous reviewer comments
   - Editorial decision comments

### Submitting a Revision

1. Prepare your revised manuscript (PDF)
2. Upload the new manuscript
3. Add a **Response Letter** (optional but recommended):
   - Address each reviewer comment
   - Explain changes made
   - Justify any unchanged items
4. Click "Submit Revision"

### Revision Tips

- **Be thorough**: Address every reviewer comment
- **Be specific**: Reference page/line numbers for changes
- **Be professional**: Thank reviewers for constructive feedback
- **Highlight changes**: Use track changes or colored text in manuscript

## After Acceptance

### What Happens Next

1. The Admin/Editor assigns your article to an Issue
2. Publication details are added (page numbers, DOI)
3. The article is published on the public site
4. You receive a notification with the article link

### Accessing Your Published Article

- View it on the public site: `/articles/[id]`
- Share the DOI link with colleagues
- Reference it in future work

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long does review take?**
- Initial review: 1-2 weeks
- Peer review: 4-6 weeks
- Decision: Within 1 week of review completion

**Can I withdraw my submission?**
- Contact the editorial office with your request

**Can I submit to another journal simultaneously?**
- No, UJ requires exclusive submission

**How do I add co-authors after submission?**
- Contact the editorial office

**What if I disagree with reviewer comments?**
- Respectfully address concerns in your response letter
- Provide scientific justification for your position
- The editor will consider your response

**When will my article be published?**
- After acceptance, publication timing depends on issue scheduling
- Typically within 1-3 months of acceptance
